# Poolula LLC Resources

## Colorado LLC Compliance

### State of Colorado
- [Colorado Secretary of State - Business Search](https://www.coloradosos.gov/biz/BusinessEntityCriteriaExt.do) - Verify LLC status and annual reports
- [Colorado Periodic Report Filing](https://www.sos.state.co.us/biz/FilePeriodicReport.do) - File annual periodic reports (due annually)
- [Colorado Business Express](https://www.coloradobusinessexpress.com/) - One-stop business registration

### Federal Tax & Compliance
- [IRS Business Tax Information](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed) - Federal tax guidance for LLCs
- [IRS Form 1065](https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-form-1065) - Partnership return (if LLC elects partnership taxation)
- [IRS Schedule E](https://www.irs.gov/forms-pubs/about-schedule-e-form-1040) - Supplemental income and loss (rental property)
- [Get Transcript Online](https://www.irs.gov/individuals/get-transcript) - Request IRS transcripts
